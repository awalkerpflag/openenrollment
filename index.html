Open Enrollment

Open enrollment is your once-a-year opportunity for making changes to your Health, Dental, Vision and Life Insurance coverage options.

  • All changes must be submitted by June 14 and changes are effective on July 1st.
  • Please note that transit, parking, retirement and Flexible Spending Accounts are not affected by the Open Enrollment period. For more on those benefits, see the Benefits Portal

    Dental Coverage

    United Concordia Advantage Plus Network

    The plan includes fully covered preventative services (e.g. cleanings) twice a year at in-network providers. Filings, crowns, extractions, surgery and other procedures are covered to a maximum benefits of $2,000 per year (excluding preventative services).

    Non-network providers are paid at percentages of the "MAC" (maximum allowable charge) depending on the service performed. You are responsible for the difference between the amount covered and the amount billed by the dentist. When you utilize an in-network provider, the provider agrees to accept payment at the rates offered United Concordia resulting in a significant savings for you.

    As an added bonus, in-network providers will file your claims for you!

    You can find more about covered dental services in the Health Plan Book

    Use United Concordia's My Dental Benefits to Find a dentist or specialist, print out replacement dental cards, access claim forms and check claim status online.

    Compare in and out-of-network benefits in the Dental Benefit Comparison

    Thinking About Adding a Domestic Partner, Spouse, Child or Dependent?

    PFLAG will pay for half the additional premium over the single rate with the additional being paid through pre-tax payroll deduction.

    Math is hard.

    So don't do it. We're happy to do it for you. Just contact David or Adam to arrange for a consultation.

    For example, the Choice High single plan is $691.77. The employee and spouse plan is $1,335.05. The difference is $643.28. PFLAG would contribute $321.64 (50%) and your portion would also be $321.64 (which is $148.45 per bi-weekly payroll). See the rate sheet for all rates but remember, we'll do all the math for you.

    Domestic Partnerships

    For employees who enroll their domestic partners under our health insurance plan and are subject to additional imputed income tax, PFLAG will gross-up the employee's salaries to the level where such employees will bear no more tax consequences than married employees who opt to cover their spouses.

    In addition to enrollment forms, you must complete the Domestic Partnership Affidavit along with your enrollment forms.
